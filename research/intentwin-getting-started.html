01 — Account Setup

Sign up & onboarding

Create your account and complete the 5-step wizard to configure your company profile.

1

Create your account

Visit intentwin.com, enter your work email, and set a password. You'll provide your name and organization.

2

Company profile

Enter your company name, a short description, and select your primary industry.

3

Add differentiators

Add 3 things that make you unique. These get woven into "Why Us" sections automatically.

4

Knowledge base

Pick your proposal types (Cloud Migration, AI/ML, etc.) or skip and upload documents later.

5

You're ready

Review your setup and jump straight into creating your first proposal.

Pro tip

Don't worry about perfection. You can update your company profile, differentiators, and knowledge base anytime from Settings.

02 — New Proposal

Start a new proposal

Click "New Proposal" from your dashboard and choose how to input your opportunity.

📁

Upload files

Upload an RFP, client brief, or email. Supports PDF, DOCX, PPTX, and TXT.

📋

Paste content

Paste email threads, meeting notes, or a brief directly into the text area.

💬

Describe it

Describe the opportunity in your own words. Great for early-stage conversations.

✍️

Manual entry

Skip AI extraction and fill in form fields yourself.

Client research

Enable the "Research client" checkbox and the AI will gather company intelligence to enrich your proposal automatically.

Review & create

Final checkpoint before the AI starts writing.

1

Review the summary

Check pain points, desired outcomes, and win strategy themes.

2

Check the "I approve this Intent" checkbox to confirm the AI's direction.

3

Why approval matters

07 — Quality

Quality review

Three independent AI judges review your proposal like a real evaluation committee.

📝

Content

Clarity, depth, and persuasiveness

🎯

Client Fit

Addresses specific challenges

📊

Evidence

Claims backed by data

🎤

Brand Voice

Tone & style consistency

Each section gets a score out of 10. Sections below 8.5 are flagged for auto-remediation. The council provides consensus indicators, specific feedback, and one-click rewrites.

Annotations

In review mode, highlight any text to add your own comments. They're tracked alongside the council's feedback and can be resolved individually.

10 — Knowledge

Knowledge base

Two layers work together to make proposals smarter.

🔒 L1: Company Truth

Verified, structured data — your profile, certifications, products, capabilities, and case studies. The "source of truth" for every claim.

📚 L2: Document Library

Uploaded documents — past proposals, white papers, capability statements. Chunked and indexed for AI-powered retrieval during generation.

Best documents to upload

Start with winning past proposals, capability statements, case studies, and team bios. Supports PDF, DOCX, PPTX, and TXT up to 50MB.
