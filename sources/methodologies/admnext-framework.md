## Overview

**ADMnext** is Capgemini's full-stack application development and maintenance (ADM) service framework. It focuses on business imperatives to reduce costs, improve efficiency, modernize IT landscapes, and enable new technology adoption.

## Four Service Layers

### Layer 1: Adaptive Services

**Purpose**: Flexibility and responsiveness across the full development lifecycle

**Scope**:
- Business processes
- Applications platforms
- Hybrid infrastructure landscapes

**Lifecycle Coverage**:
- Transition
- Build
- Support
- Decommissioning

**Benefits**:
- Easier-to-consume IT service aligned to business outcomes
- Frees up time and budget for transformation
- Flexibility to respond to change

### Layer 2: Modernization Services

**Purpose**: Continuously remodel IT landscape to reduce technical debt

**Commitment**: 20% or more reduction in IT landscape through:
- Rationalization
- Modernization
- Migration

**Deliverables**:
- Vision and roadmap development
- Technical debt reduction plan
- Complexity and risk reduction
- Increased landscape flexibility and agility

### Layer 3: Business Insightful Services

**Purpose**: Align IT portfolio with business objectives

**Focus Areas**:
- Progressive business process improvements
- Industry experience application
- Business KPI monitoring
- Outcome-focused IT delivery

**Benefits**:
- Better understanding of IT business value
- IT aligned to business outcomes
- Industry-specific insights

### Layer 4: Emerging Products & Services

**Purpose**: Bring innovation into the heart of the organization

**Approach**:
- Continuous, incremental innovation
- Latest thinking from across Capgemini Group
- Risk mitigation for security and data fragmentation

**Focus**:
- New technology adoption
- Innovation pipelines
- Emerging capability development
