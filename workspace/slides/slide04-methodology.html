Intent-Driven Development (IDD)

A 3-Layer Context Model for Trustworthy AI Generation

L1

Company Truth

Verified capabilities, certifications, case studies, and methodologies. The canonical source of what we can claim.

L2

Proposal Intent

Client outcomes, win strategy, constraints, and success metrics. Human-defined goals that guide generation.

L3

Generated Content

AI-created proposal sections, reviewed and refined by humans. Every claim traceable to L1 sources.

KEY DIFFERENTIATOR

Every claim in every proposal is traceable to verified company sources
