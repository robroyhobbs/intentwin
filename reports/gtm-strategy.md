# ProposalAI Go-To-Market Strategy

> First 10 Customers Playbook
> Target: $10K MRR in 90 days

## Strategic Foundation

### Positioning Statement
For mid-market companies who struggle with RFP response quality and team coordination, ProposalAI is the AI proposal platform that delivers enterprise-grade accuracy at mid-market pricing. Unlike AutogenAI (too expensive) or Arphie (questionnaires only), we combine narrative excellence with transparent AI that shows its sources.

### Value Proposition Canvas

**Customer Jobs:**
1. Respond to RFPs faster (hours not days)
2. Maintain quality across team members
3. Win more deals with less effort
4. Keep content library current
5. Satisfy compliance requirements

**Pains:**
- AI hallucinations in proposals
- Cross-team coordination chaos
- Manual content library maintenance
- Enterprise tools too expensive
- SMB tools lack critical features

**Gains:**
- 84%+ first-pass acceptance
- Source citations for compliance
- Real-time collaboration
- Transparent, predictable pricing

### Channel 3: LinkedIn Outreach (Ongoing)
**Investment:** $0 (organic)
**Target:** 50 connections/day → 2-3 trials/week

**ICP Targeting:**
- Title: "Proposal Manager", "RFP Manager", "Sales Engineer", "Pre-Sales"
- Company size: 50-500 employees
- Industries: Tech, Consulting, Government contractors

**Outreach Sequence:**
1. **Connect Request** (no pitch)
   ```
   Hi [Name], noticed you're leading proposal efforts at [Company].
   I'm building tools for proposal teams - would love to connect.
   ```

2. **Value-First Message** (Day 2)
   ```
   Thanks for connecting! Quick question - what's your biggest
   frustration with your current RFP process? Trying to understand
   the landscape.
   ```

3. **Soft Offer** (Day 4, if engaged)
   ```
   Based on what you shared, you might find [specific feature] helpful.
   We're offering early access to proposal teams willing to give
   feedback. Interested in a 15-min walkthrough?
   ```

**Metrics:**
- 50 connections/day = 250/week
- 10% reply rate = 25 conversations
- 20% demo rate = 5 demos/week
- 40% trial rate = 2 trials/week

## Sales Process

### Trial Flow
1. **Signup** (self-service)
   - Email + Company name + Industry
   - Auto-create organization
   - Start 14-day trial (3 proposals)

2. **Onboarding** (automated)
   - Welcome email with quick-start guide
   - In-app wizard: Company → Industry → First proposal
   - Day 1: First proposal created

3. **Activation** (automated)
   - Day 2: Check if proposal exported
   - Day 4: Email if no activity
   - Day 7: Mid-trial check-in
   - Day 10: Upgrade reminder
   - Day 13: Trial ending warning

4. **Conversion** (semi-automated)
   - High-engagement trials: Personal email
   - Low-engagement: Automated extension offer
   - Objection handling: ROI calculator

### Pricing Psychology
- Anchor: "Enterprise accuracy" (implies $30K+ value)
- Comparison: "1/4 the cost of AutogenAI"
- Risk reversal: "14-day trial, no credit card"
- Urgency: "50% off first year" (for early adopters)

**Backup:** Extend trials, add freemium tier
